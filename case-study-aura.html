The Challenge

Talented creatives trapped in a forgettable brand.

Aura Studio had exceptional work but their brand identity screamed "2015 freelancer." They were losing enterprise proposals to agencies half as talented but twice as polished. We needed a visual system that matched the quality of their output.

Our Process

From invisible to unforgettable.

01

Brand Archaeology

We dug deep into what made Aura unique — their processes, personality, and values. Stakeholder interviews and competitor positioning analysis.

02

Visual Identity

Created a bold mark built around overlapping circles representing collaboration. Defined a premium color system and typography hierarchy.

03

System Rollout

Delivered a 120-page brand guidelines document, redesigned their website, templated pitch decks, and unified all touchpoints in 8 weeks.
