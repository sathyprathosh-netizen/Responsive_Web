The Challenge

When milliseconds mean everything.

PulseHealth needed to replace a decade-old patient monitoring system that crashed under load and couldn't handle real-time data from IoT wearables. Nurses were spending more time fighting the software than caring for patients. We built a platform where every vital sign updates in under 200ms — because in healthcare, lag isn't just annoying, it's dangerous.

Our Process

Built for the ward, not the boardroom.

01

Clinical Shadowing

Our team spent 3 weeks in ICU wards watching nurses interact with existing systems. We mapped every friction point and designed around real clinical workflows.

02

FHIR-First Architecture

Built on HL7 FHIR standards for seamless interoperability. Ingests data from 40+ IoT wearable models and integrates with every major EHR system.

03

Intelligent Alerts

ML-powered alert system that reduces alarm fatigue by 70%. Smart triage separates critical events from noise so nurses respond to what matters.
