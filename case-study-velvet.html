Client Velvet & Void
Timeline 4 Months
Role Full Stack, Motion
Stack Next.js, GSAP, Shopify
The Challenge

Luxury fashion deserves more than a product grid.

Velvet & Void is a haute couture brand that was losing online sales to competitors with more immersive digital experiences. Their existing Shopify store felt generic — identical to every other fashion brand. They needed a digital flagship that conveyed the same emotion as walking into their Milan boutique.

Our Process

From catalog to catwalk.

01

Brand Immersion

We spent two weeks at V&V's Milan HQ photographing their atelier, studying their fabric textures, and absorbing the brand DNA to translate it into pixels.

02

Motion Language

We crafted a custom motion system using GSAP — every page transition mirrors the fluidity of silk. Product reveals use parallax layers inspired by editorial layouts.

03

Shopify Integration

Custom Headless Shopify build using Next.js for blazing performance. Inventory synced in real-time with their Milan and Tokyo warehouses.
