NeoBank Interface Design

Redefining digital banking for the Gen Z demographic — faster, cleaner, and built for mobile-first.

Client FinCorp
Timeline 3 Months
Role UI/UX, Dev
Stack React, Node.js
The Challenge

Legacy banks weren't built for the swipe generation.

Traditional banking apps are cluttered, slow, and uninspiring. FinCorp needed a mobile-first experience that felt as intuitive as social media but functioned with bank-grade security. Users were dropping off at onboarding — a 68% abandonment rate that was costing millions.

Our Process

Three phases. Zero guesswork.

01

Discovery & Research

We interviewed 50 real users to map pain points. Competitive audit of 12 neobanks revealed critical UX gaps no one was addressing.

02

Prototyping & Testing

Low-fidelity wireframes focused on transaction speed and balance clarity. We iterated through 4 rounds of user testing before committing to pixels.

03

High-Fidelity & Dev

Introduced the "Midnight" dark theme to reduce eye strain. Built with React for performance and Node.js for real-time transaction feeds.
